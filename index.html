01 Our Approach
A radiotheranostic platform designed around endogenous biology, not engineered around it.
●

Clinically Validated Biology

Our approach uses a clinically validated liver-targeting biology as the foundation for payload delivery.

●

Theranostic Architecture

A single platform designed for both imaging and therapeutic delivery. Diagnostic imaging informs treatment; treatment efficacy is measurable through the same mechanism.

●

Modular Expansion

The platform architecture is designed to enable expansion into additional liver malignancies beyond the lead indication — a pipeline-in-a-platform approach.
